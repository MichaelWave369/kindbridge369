# KindBridge369 v0.7 — Follow-Up Kit

v0.7 adds printable follow-up tools for the moment after someone finds a possible next step.

The problem is simple: people often make a call, visit an office, ask a helper, or read a notice, then lose the thread because life is stressful. KindBridge should not only help someone find the next step. It should help them remember what happened next.

## Added pages

- `public/print/contact-log.html`
- `public/print/appointment-notes.html`
- `public/print/document-checklist.html`

These pages are linked from the Print Shelf at:

- `public/print/index.html`

## Design rule

Keep follow-up notes short, useful, and respectful.

A good note answers:

- Who did I contact?
- When did it happen?
- What did they say?
- What do I need to do next?
- What should I bring or gather?

A bad note collects private details that do not help the next step.

## Why this matters

Follow-up is where many people get lost.

The Follow-Up Kit turns help into a small receipt trail: not surveillance, not bureaucracy, just enough memory to keep moving.
