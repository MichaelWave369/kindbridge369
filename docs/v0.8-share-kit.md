# KindBridge369 v0.8 — Share Kit

v0.8 adds printable discovery materials for helping KindBridge leave the browser and show up where people already gather.

## Why this matters

A help tool is only useful when people can find it at the moment they need it. Many real-world support paths begin at a bulletin board, library desk, church office, family table, RV park, school lobby, community meetup, or neighbor conversation.

The Share Kit creates simple, printable entry points that point people back to the free Print Shelf.

## Added pages

- `public/print/community-flyer.html` — a full-page flyer for boards, desks, and public tables.
- `public/print/pocket-cards.html` — cut-out cards that can be shared or placed in folders.
- `public/print/table-sign.html` — a large sign for help tables and local resource stations.

## Design rules

- No login required.
- No payment required.
- No tracking required.
- Keep language plain and warm.
- Point people to practical next-step tools.
- Do not promise outcomes.
- Keep discovery materials easy to print in black-and-white if needed.

## Core principle

The map should be easy to find before someone is already overwhelmed.
