KindBridge369 Town Starter Kit

Start a local help map.

Use this page to begin a simple, verified list for your town, church, library, school, workplace, RV park, or neighborhood table.

Start with six buckets

  • Food and basic supplies
  • Utility or bill support
  • ID, forms, and paperwork
  • Elder tech and scam prevention
  • Pet food, low-cost care, lost pets
  • Rides, laundry, showers, library help

Verification rule

  • Call or check the source directly.
  • Write the date checked.
  • Write what is unknown.
  • Do not promise eligibility.
  • Refresh the list on a schedule.

Plain script

I am helping build a simple local help list. Can you confirm your hours, what kind of help you offer, who can ask, and what someone should bring?

Kind boundary

This list helps people find doors to knock on. It should not pretend to decide outcomes, replace professionals, or guarantee service.

Best first version: one page, ten verified entries, checked monthly. Small and true beats big and stale.
