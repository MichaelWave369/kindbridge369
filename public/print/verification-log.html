Checked beats guessed.

Use this page when building or updating a local help list. The goal is simple: write what was checked, when it was checked, and what remains unknown.

Kind rule: local details can change. A resource with an old or guessed detail can waste someone’s time. Date every check and leave blanks when you do not know.

Good details to verify

  • Hours and days
  • Address or service area
  • Best contact route
  • What to bring

Mark unknowns

  • Eligibility unclear
  • Phone not answered
  • Website outdated
  • Needs another check

Keep it kind

  • No gossip notes
  • No extra private details
  • No promises on behalf of others
  • No shame language
